证明： $\sum_{1\le i\le n}i^2=\frac {n(n+1)(2n+1)} {6}$

#### 归纳法

当n=1时， $1^2=\frac {1\times (1+1)\times (2\times 1+1)} {6}=1$，成立

当n=k+1时，有
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sum_{1\le i\le k+1} i^2&=\sum_{1\le i\le k} i^2+(k+1)^2 \\
&=\frac {k(k+1)(2k+1)} {6}+(k+1)^2 \\
&=\frac {(k+1)[k(2k+1)+6(k+1)]} {6} \\
&=\frac {(k+1)[2k^2+k+6k+6]} {6} \\
&=\frac {(k+1)[2k^2+7k+6]} {6} \\ %十字相乘法
&=\frac {(k+1)(k+2)(2k+3)} {6} \\
&=\frac {(k+1)[(k+1)+1][2(k+1)+1]} {6}
\end{aligned}
$$
故得证。
